About Me

After 7 years of owning and operating a small business in the grocery/coffee world I sold my company to learn the skills needed to be a Full Stack Developer. I have a passion for building beautiful, functional creations, whether it's on the web or in my woodshop. I am based in Minneapolis and I love living in the Twin Cities. If I'm not eating dinner at one of the amazing restaurants we have in MN you can find me running the lakes, playing frisbee golf, kicking a soccer ball around, or snowboarding in the winter.

I'll finish with a quote (I know, it's lame) that has inspired me for years. A constant reminder that if I don't push myself to be the best I can, the next person will.

"All men/women dream, but not equally" -T.E. Lawrence
